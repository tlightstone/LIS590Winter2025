Update 8

As I finish week 8 at my internship in the Private Family Archives, I’ve noticed more patterns when it comes to how DVDs are getting read or not read. I have moved on to some other formats such as photos, word documents, and audio files. I’ve also learned about more formats of photos. Most of what I am looking at are JPGs but there are also TIFs and BMPs. The audio files also come in a number of formats. Such as CDA, WAV, and WMV. It’s been helpful to add columns to our spreadsheet for better organization of the metadata that I am recording. Additionally, I am keeping my document of notes about the metadata up to date to explain the choices that I’m making as well as trying to explain why certain things are happening. We’ve noticed a discrepancy between certain DVDs and their playability and file properties access depending on if it is being read by a Windows or Mac operating system. I enjoy figuring out why things are happening by reading through forums online.
